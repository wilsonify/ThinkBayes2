
auto_gau.f   Produces the K=17 files acor.d and at_int.d for 
             correlated Gaussian data.

uncor_gau.f  Produces the K=17 files uacor.d and aut_int.d for 
             uncorrelated Gaussian data. 

bauto.f      Produces the K=17 binning estimates of the integrated 
             autocorrelation time for the correlated Gaussian data 
             on the file b_aint.d.

Gnuplots with acor.plt and at_int.plt.
