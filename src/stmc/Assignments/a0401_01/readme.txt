
auto_g17.f   A variant of auto_gau.f. Produces files acor.d and 
             acor.small for 2**17 correlated Gaussian data.

uncor_g17.f  A variant of unco_gau.f. Produces the file uacor.d
             for 2**17 uncorrelated Gaussian data. 

Put in by hand: yaxis.small data file.

gnuplot ac17.plt plots the results.
