
at21.f    Variant of auto_gau.f. Produces the K=21 files acor21.d 
          and at_int21.d for correlated gaussian data.

un21.f    Variant of uncor_gau.f. Produces the K=21 files uacor21.d 
          and uat_int21.d for uncorrelated gaussian data.

ba21.f    Variant of bauto.f. Produces the K=21 binning estimates 
          of the integrated autocorrelation time.

Plot with at1.plt.

The following results are shifted to fit all in one figure:

at17a.f   Variant of auto_gau.f. Produces the K=17 files acor17a.d 
          and at_int17a.d for correlated gaussian data.

ba17a.f   Variant of bauto.f. Produces the K=17 binning estimates 
          of the integrated autocorrelation time.

at14a.f   Variant of auto_gau.f. Produces the K=14 files acor14a.d 
          and at_int14a.d for correlated gaussian data.

ba14a.f   Variant of bauto.f. Produces the K=14 binning estimates 
          of the integrated autocorrelation time.

Plot all together with at_int.plt.
